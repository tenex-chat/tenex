# Task Drag-and-Drop Reordering

## Drag-and-Drop Reordering

Tasks in the day view support drag-and-drop reordering; the first drag on a day locks in manual order, and before that the smart time-sort runs automatically. New items created after a manual drag are appended at the bottom with maxSortIndex + 1. [^c6a12-13]
