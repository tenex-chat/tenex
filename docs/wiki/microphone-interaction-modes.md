# Microphone Interaction Modes

## Interaction Modes

Short tap on the microphone button opens the recording sheet, records, and closes the sheet immediately when done; long press opens the recording sheet, records, and keeps the sheet open to show agent activity and allow continued interaction. [^c6a12-12]
